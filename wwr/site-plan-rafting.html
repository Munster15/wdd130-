Overview

Purpose

Building revenue while bringing families together without the influence of technology.

Audience

My audience is families with kids over the age of 8, from a medium to high income bracket. The like adventure, the outdoors, and white water rafting. They want family time, a disconnect from devices, laughs, and memories, and are lacking time together. They will usually access my site through laptops and mobile devices.
